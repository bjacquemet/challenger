Our offer

Stack the odds in your favour.

Gain time

The right level

We make sure all our challenges are adapted to the candidate's level and skills. Junior, Intermediate or Senior, make sure you are testing for the right level. View a project.

Actionable

Entertaining and realistic

How it works

Let's team up: we verify candidate's skills and level, you check the fit with your company.

Step 1: choose the right challenge

Choose our challenge or we will design a challenge tailored to your needs – level of the candidate, technologies.

Step 2: coding

We will provide the code and the instructions to the candidate, and deal with her potential programming questions in the process.

Step 3: Report and compare

After the candidate has finished the challenge and submitted the code, we will correct it and provide you with a detailes reports and the candidate's code.
